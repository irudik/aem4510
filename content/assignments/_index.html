Grading

5 Problem sets (50%): 12.5% each, lowest-scoring problem set dropped

2 Prelims (35%): 20% on your highest-scoring prelim, 15% on your lowest-scoring prelim

1 Final project (15%)

Problem sets

You will have 5 problem sets. You may consult with me or the TAs during office hours. Students taking the class at the 5000 level will have extra problems in each problem set.

When are they due?

  • Tentative problem set due dates: XYZ
  • Problem sets are due on 11:59PM on the listed due date.
  • No late problem sets will be accepted.

Who can I work with, how do I submit them?

  • You may work in groups of up to 3 on problem sets.
  • On Canvas, submit one problem set per group with all group members names on it.
  • If you are doing the problem set on pen and paper you must scan and convert it into a pdf using something like Adobe Scan. Pictures will not be accepted.

Prelims

When are the prelims?

  • Tentative prelim dates: March 21, May 9 (all day)
  • The prelims are in class during our regular class time

What do the prelims cover?

  • The first will largely cover sections 01 to 09, the second will cover the rest of the course.
  • The prelims will have questions based on the slides and readings assigned.
